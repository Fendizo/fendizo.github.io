      About

      Here is a great supercharged collection of about 2000 emoticons and emoji symbols ready to use on Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, Skype and other web sites, chats and messengers. Just find emoji picture you want and click or tap on it to copy it to the clipboard. After that switch to another website or application and paste it like an any other text. Even if symbol looks like a black square or question mark, it most probably will be converted into appropriate image by web site or application where you paste it.

      Some of cool features:

      • two input methods: emoji keyboard or copy and paste emoji;
      • all new emoji of 2016 are here;
      • secret and forbidden emojis (like middle finger) are here;
      • all emoji are grouped into categories (hands, hearts, smiley faces, arrows, etc);
      • instant search of icon by name or tags;
      • automatic copy into clipboard by click;
      • changeable emoji sizes;
      • emoji meanings (names) are displayed for each item;
      • works on desktop and mobile;
      • multiple design themes;
      • no software installation required;
      • most popular emoji list at the top of page;
      • you can use this emoticons in facebook status, comments and chat;
      • emoji works on all popular web sites.
